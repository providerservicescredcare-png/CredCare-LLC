About Us

We are a dedicated contracting and credentialing company specializing in supporting healthcare providers throughout their enrollment and credentialing journey. Our team assists providers with enrolling in major healthcare organizations, including IPA’s, Medical Groups, MSOs, and Health Plans.

In addition to enrollment, we also manage ongoing provider credential maintenance and compliance, ensuring that all records remain accurate, updated, and aligned with industry standards.

Our founder, Vijay Ravichandran, brings over 10 years of experience in the healthcare credentialing and contracting field. Under his leadership, we are committed to delivering efficient, reliable, and compliant credentialing solutions that allow providers to focus on what matters most—patient care.
