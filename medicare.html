Medicare & Medicaid (Medi-Cal)

Certification Support

We assist healthcare groups and individual providers in becoming certified with Medicare and Medicaid (Medi-Cal). Our team manages the complete enrollment process to ensure accuracy, compliance, and timely approval.

NPPES (NPI) Creation & Management

We help providers create and manage their NPPES (National Provider Identifier) credentials, including applying for new NPI numbers, updating existing NPI information, and ensuring proper linkage between individual and group profiles.

Medicare Enrollment Applications (CMS Forms 855)

We prepare and submit all required Medicare enrollment applications on behalf of groups and individual providers, including:

  • CMS 855B – Enrollment for group practices and clinics
  • CMS 855I – Enrollment for individual practitioners
  • CMS 855R – Reassignment of Medicare benefits to group practices

Our team ensures all applications are completed accurately, required documents are included, and the process moves smoothly from submission to approval.
